U1 Definition                                                              
AS Exchange rate used for the translation between foreign currency and     
   local currency.                                                         
U1 Procedure                                                               
AS In entry transactions, you only specify the exchange rate if you want a 
   different rate to the table rate.                                       
U1 Dependencies                                                            
AS If you use the option to enter the local currency and the foreign       
   currency amount manually when you are entering an item, a different     
   exchange rate may result than that in the "Exchange Rate" field.
